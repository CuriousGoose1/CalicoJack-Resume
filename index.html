John "Calico Jack" Rackham

Summary

A pirate captain, probably of English nationality, operating in the Bahamas and Jamaica during the early 18th century. Active in 1720, towards the end of the Golden Age of Piracy, and most remembered for having two female pirate crew members: Anne Bonny and Mary Read.
